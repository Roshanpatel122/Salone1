About New Fascia

A Beauty Destination
Built On Trust

New Fascia Saloon was built around a simple idea — that every woman and every family in Sagar deserves a salon experience that feels personal, hygienic and genuinely premium, not rushed or transactional.

From everyday hair and skin care to bridal-ready transformations, our stylists and beauty professionals bring trained expertise, patience and an eye for detail to every chair. Each visit is treated as a consultation first — understanding your skin, your hair, your occasion — before any service begins.

Across our two studio branches in Tilli and Makroniya, the same standard of care, comfort and craftsmanship follows you — making New Fascia the salon families in Sagar return to, and recommend.
